# FU TV – FAQ

## What is FU TV?
FU TV is a Vancouver-based online tv station (website) that curates and produces local content — including shows, music videos, short films, and art made in and around Vancouver.

## Who runs FU TV?
FU TV is curated by James LeBuke. He also works with Faliureunit Productions.

## Can I submit my own content?
Yes. Anyone in or connected to Vancouver can submit their work to possibly be featured on FU TV.

## Will it for sure make it on?
No. but maybe.

## What kind of content do you feature?
Everything with a focus on creative, original, and local voices. old and new.

## Do I keep my monetization?
Yes. FU TV streams content through curated youtube playlists, so creators keep full ownership, views, and monetization from their videos.

## Can I customize my own FU TV page?
Yes. Every visitor can create their own version of the FU TV site. You can start from scratch, make files, and customize your personal page to explore and organize the website your own way.

## Can I suggest changes or features for the website?
Sure. In the submissions you can also include suggestions for what you’d like to see added or improved on the site. May or may not happen but can’t hurt.

## Are there other features besides videos?
Yes. FU TV also includes games and other stuff.

## How do I watch?
Click the Tubestream window. If you closed it, you can open it again from the Tube Stream app in the Start menu of this site.
